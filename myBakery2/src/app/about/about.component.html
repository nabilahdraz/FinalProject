About

The Aesthetic Bakery opened in downtown Kuala Lumpur. We bake our cakes from scratch using the finest ingredient. Our treats are as deliciously wholesome as they are beautifully decorated. The Aesthetic Bakery offers a homestyle bakery experience in the Kuala Lumpur area. Our vision is to create upscale, quick-serve bakery with a focus on simple and satisfying souther style desserts and baked goods. Our mission is simple to service for our customers at the lowest prices possible. Our ingredients are high quality,all-natural ingredients and 100% Halal.
